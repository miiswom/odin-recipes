Apple Crumble Pie

Description

A delicious and very easy Apple Crumble Pie recipe which will take you back to the good old days.

This will pair up nicely with a scoop of vanilla ice cream (or any other ice cream taste) and some custard cream.

Ingredients

  • 1 (9 inch) deep dish pie crust
  • 5 cups apples - peeled, cored and thinly sliced
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ¾ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • 6 tablespoons butter

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C.)
  2. Arrange apple slices in unbaked pie shell. Mix 1/2 cup sugar and cinnamon; sprinkle over apples.
  3. Mix 1/3 cup sugar with flour; cut in butter until crumbly. Spoon mixture over apples.
  4. Bake in preheated oven until apples are soft and top is lightly browned, about 40 minutes.
